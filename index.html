🇫🇷🥖BRUNCH VIBES
French Toast
COMFORT QUICK
  • Brioche or white bread 🍞
  • Vanilla extract 🫙
  • Eggs 🥚 (2-3)
  • Splash of milk or heavy cream 🥛
  • Cinnamon 🌿
  • Butter 🧈
  • Maple syrup 🍁
  • Powdered sugar (optional)
  1. 01Whisk together eggs 🥚, a splash of milk/cream, vanilla extract, and a good pinch of cinnamon in a shallow bowl.
  2. 02Dip each bread slice in the egg mixture and let it soak for 10-15 seconds per side.
  3. 03Melt butter 🧈 in a pan on medium heat. Cook each slice 2-3 mins per side until golden brown.
  4. 04Stack, drizzle maple syrup 🍁, dust with powdered sugar. C'est magnifique! 🇫🇷
Daiiv's Tip — Brioche is the GOAT here. Heavy cream over milk makes it richer.

Garlic Tomato Chicken
HIGH PROTEIN CREAMY

  • 2 cloves garlic 🧄

🍗🧈CLASSIC 🇮🇳
Butter Chicken
HIGH PROTEIN RICH
  • Chicken breast 🍗
  • Tomatoes 🍅 (3)
  • Cashews 🪙
  • Butter 🧈
  • Heavy cream 🥛
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger & Garlic 🧄

  • Salt 🧂

Meetu's Zooopaaa

  • Chicken thigh (boneless) 🍗
  • Buttermilk (or milk + lemon) 🥛
  • Flour + cornstarch
  • Paprika, garlic powder, cayenne
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  • Brioche bun 🍔
  • Pickles 🥒
  • Coleslaw or lettuce 🥬
  • Spicy mayo (mayo + sriracha)
  • Oil for frying

  5. 05Toast brioche bun 🍔. Spread spicy mayo. Add pickles 🥒, lettuce 🥬, and that crispy legend of a chicken piece. 🏆

01
🥔🧈CLASSIC SIDE
Mash Potatoes
CREAMY COMFORT 🤎
  • Potatoes 🥔 (as many as needed)
  • Heavy whipping cream 🥛
  • Cheese 🧀 (shredded, your choice)
  • Salt 🧂 & Pepper
  • Fresh rosemary 🌿
  1. 01Boil potatoes 🥔 whole in salted water until completely tender — a fork should go in with zero resistance. About 20–25 mins.
  2. 02Drain and peel while still hot. The skin comes off easily at this stage.
  3. 03Mash the potatoes thoroughly — no lumps. Use a masher or fork, not a blender (that makes them gluey).
  4. 04Pour in heavy whipping cream 🥛 gradually, mixing as you go until you reach your preferred consistency — silky and smooth.
  5. 05Fold in shredded cheese 🧀 and season generously with salt 🧂 and pepper. Taste and adjust.
  6. 06Top with a sprig of fresh rosemary 🌿 and serve hot alongside your main dish.
Daiiv's Tip — Warm the cream before adding it — cold cream makes the mash stiff and dense. Hot cream keeps everything fluffy. 🤌
