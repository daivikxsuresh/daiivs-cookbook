French Toast
🥚 Brunch Vibes
🍞 Comfort ⚡ Quick
  • Brioche or white bread 🍞
  • Vanilla extract 🫙
  • Eggs 🥚 (2-3)
  • Splash of milk or heavy cream 🥛
  • Cinnamon 🌿
  • Butter 🧈
  • Maple syrup 🍁
  • Powdered sugar (optional)
  1. 1Whisk together eggs 🥚, a splash of milk/cream, vanilla extract, and a good pinch of cinnamon in a shallow bowl.
  2. 2Dip each bread slice in the egg mixture and let it soak for 10-15 seconds per side.
  3. 3Melt butter 🧈 in a pan on medium heat. Cook each slice 2-3 mins per side until golden brown.
  4. 4Stack, drizzle maple syrup 🍁, dust with powdered sugar. C'est magnifique! 🇫🇷
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Brioche is the GOAT here. Heavy cream over milk makes it richer.

Avocado Toast
🌶️ Spicy Twist
💚 Healthy Fats ⚡ Quick
  • Bread (any) 🍞
  • Avocado 🥑
  • Jalapeños 🌶️
  • Onions 🧅
  • Green chili 🌿
  • Garlic 🧄
  • Amul cheese 🧀
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  • Olive oil 🫒
  1. 1Sauté diced jalapeños 🌶️, onions 🧅, green chili, and minced garlic 🧄 in olive oil until softened and fragrant.

  4. 4Pile on the sautéed veggies and finish with grated Amul cheese 🧀. Eat immediately.
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Add a fried egg on top for extra protein. Squeeze of lime if you have it 🍋.

Garlic Tomato Chicken
🧀 Boursin Special
💪 High Protein 🧀 Creamy
  • Chicken breast 🍗
  • Boursin cheese 🧀
  • 3 tomatoes 🍅
  • 2 cloves garlic 🧄
  • Butter 🧈
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  1. 1Season chicken breast generously with salt, pepper, and paprika on both sides.
  2. 2Sear chicken in butter 🧈 on medium-high heat, 4-5 mins per side until golden. Set aside.
  3. 3In the same pan, sauté minced garlic 🧄. Add diced tomatoes 🍅. Cook 5 mins until softened.
  4. 4Add Boursin cheese 🧀 and stir until melted into a creamy sauce. Return chicken and simmer 5 mins.
  5. 5Serve over rice or with crusty bread 🍞. The sauce is everything. 🤌
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Butterfly the chicken breast so it cooks evenly. No dry chicken allowed in this kitchen.

Butter Chicken
🇮🇳 Classic
💪 High Protein 🍛 Rich
  • Chicken breast 🍗
  • Tomatoes 🍅 (3)
  • Cashews 🪙
  • Butter 🧈
  • Heavy cream 🥛
  • Garam masala
  • Ginger & Garlic 🧄
  • Cumin, turmeric, chili powder
  • Salt 🧂
  • Greek yogurt (marinade)
  1. 1Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, garam masala and salt. At least 30 mins or overnight.
  2. 2Cook the chicken in butter 🧈 until charred on edges. Set aside.
  3. 3Blend tomatoes 🍅 and soaked cashews together until smooth. This is your secret weapon.
  4. 4In the same pan, melt butter, add spices (cumin, turmeric), then pour in the blended sauce. Cook 10 mins.
  5. 5Add heavy cream 🥛, return chicken, simmer 10 more mins. Serve with naan or rice. 🙏
💡 Daiiv's Tip: The cashews make it thick and creamy without too much cream. Don't skip them.

Keema Chicken & Rice
+ Hot Cheetos on the side 🔴
💪 High Protein ⚡ Simple
  • Ground chicken (keema) 🍗
  • Basmati rice 🍚
  • Onion 🧅
  • Garlic & ginger 🧄
  • Green peas 🟢
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Chili powder 🌶️
  • Salt 🧂, oil 🫒
  • Hot Cheetos 🔴 (essential side)
  1. 1Cook basmati rice 🍚 as usual. Season water with a little salt and cumin for fragrant rice.
  2. 2Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle. Add diced onion and cook until golden.
  3. 3Add garlic-ginger and cook 1 min. Add ground chicken 🍗. Break it up and cook through, 7-8 mins.
  4. 4Stir in all spices + peas. Cook another 5 mins. Taste and adjust salt 🧂.
  5. 5Serve keema over rice. Bag of Hot Cheetos 🔴 on the side. This is the way. 🫡
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Squeeze lemon 🍋 over the keema before serving. The acid cuts through beautifully.

Chicken Wings — Bangalore Style
✈️ Air Fryer
💪 High Protein 🌶️ Spicy
  • Chicken drumsticks 🍗
  • Olive oil 🫒
  • Greek yogurt
  • Salt 🧂
  • Pepper
  • Chili powder 🌶️
  • Garam masala
  • Lemon 🍋
  • Ginger-garlic paste 🧄
  1. 1Mix yogurt, olive oil 🫒, ginger-garlic paste 🧄, chili powder 🌶️, garam masala, lemon 🍋, salt & pepper. This is your marinade.

Chilli Garlic Oil Pasta
💪 Protein Pasta
💪 High Protein 🌶️ Spicy
  • Protein pasta 🍝
  • Onions 🧅
  • Minced garlic 🧄
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey 🍯
  • Heavy whipping cream 🥛
  • Salt 🧂 & Pepper
  • Low fat mozzarella 🧀
  • Chili flakes 🌶️
  • Olive oil 🫒
  1. 1Cook protein pasta 🍝 al dente per package. Reserve a cup of pasta water.
  2. 2In a pan, heat olive oil 🫒 generously. Add chili flakes 🌶️ and minced garlic 🧄. Let it bloom — low heat, don't burn it.
  3. 3Add diced onions 🧅. Cook until translucent. Deglaze with soy sauce and honey 🍯. Stir well.
  4. 4Pour in heavy cream 🥛, add mozzarella 🧀, season with salt & pepper 🧂. Simmer until slightly thickened.
  5. 5Toss in the cooked pasta. Add pasta water a splash at a time to get the right saucy consistency. Serve hot. 🔥
💡 Daiiv's Tip: The honey + soy + chili combo is the secret. Sweet, salty, and spicy all at once. Chef's kiss. 🤌

Chicken Alfredo Pasta
🇮🇹 Italian Night
💪 High Protein 🥛 Creamy
  • Chicken breast 🍗
  • Fettuccine pasta 🍝
  • Heavy whipping cream 🥛
  • Parmesan 🧀
  • Butter 🧈
  • Garlic & onion powder 🧄
  • Italian seasoning 🌿
  • Paprika & Cajun
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  • Fresh basil 🌿
  1. 1Season chicken breast 🍗 with salt, pepper, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, paprika, and Cajun. Let it sit 10 mins.
  2. 2Cook pasta 🍝 al dente. Reserve pasta water.
  3. 3Sear chicken in butter 🧈 until cooked through and golden — 4-5 mins per side. Rest for 5 mins, then slice thin.
  4. 4In the same pan, melt more butter 🧈. Add heavy cream 🥛 and simmer. Add parmesan 🧀 gradually, stirring constantly.
  5. 5Toss in pasta, top with sliced chicken. Garnish with fresh basil 🌿 and cracked pepper. Done. 👨🏽‍🍳
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Never let the Alfredo sauce fully boil or it'll break. Keep it at a gentle simmer.

Garlic Butter Shrimp
⚡ 15-Min Dinner
💪 High Protein ⚡ Quick
  • Shrimp 🍤 (peeled)
  • Butter 🧈
  • Garlic (6 cloves!) 🧄
  • Lemon 🍋
  • Paprika & chili flakes 🌶️
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  • White wine (optional)
  • Crusty bread or rice 🍞
  1. 1Pat shrimp 🍤 dry. Season with paprika, salt & pepper 🧂.
  2. 2Melt butter 🧈 in a pan on medium-high. Add shrimp — don't crowd them. 1-2 mins per side until pink. Remove.
  3. 3Lower heat. Add more butter + minced garlic 🧄. Sauté 30 seconds — golden, not brown. Add chili flakes 🌶️.
  4. 4Return shrimp, squeeze lemon 🍋, toss in fresh parsley. Serve over rice or with bread to mop up the sauce. 🍞
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Don't overcook the shrimp. When they curl into a C, they're done. O-shape means overcooked.

Smash Burger
🔥 The Main Event
😤 Worth It 💪 Protein Packed
  • 80/20 ground beef 🥩
  • Brioche bun 🍔
  • American cheese 🧀
  • Butter 🧈
  • Onion (thin sliced) 🧅
  • Pickles 🥒
  • Lettuce 🥬
  • Tomato 🍅
  • Smash sauce (mayo + ketchup + mustard + pickles + garlic)
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  1. 1Form ground beef 🥩 into loosely packed 3-4oz balls. Don't compress — the loose pack is the key to the smash.
  2. 2Get a cast iron or heavy pan SCREAMING hot. Add a tiny bit of butter. Place beef ball and SMASH DOWN hard with a spatula. Hold 10 seconds.
  3. 3Season immediately with salt & pepper 🧂. Cook 2 mins. DON'T TOUCH IT. You want that crust.
  4. 4Flip, immediately top with American cheese 🧀. Add thin onion slices on top. Cover pan 30 seconds to melt cheese.
  5. 5Toast brioche bun in butter 🧈. Build: sauce → lettuce 🥬 → tomato 🍅 → pickles 🥒 → double patty stack → more sauce. 🍔
💡 Daiiv's Tip: The smash sauce is everything. Mix mayo, ketchup, yellow mustard, diced pickles, garlic powder. Make extra. Always make extra.

Crispy Chicken Sandwich
🔥 Better than Popeyes
💪 High Protein 🔥 Fan Fav
  • Chicken thigh (boneless) 🍗
  • Buttermilk (or milk + lemon) 🥛
  • Flour + cornstarch
  • Paprika, garlic powder, cayenne
  • Salt & Pepper 🧂
  • Brioche bun 🍔
  • Pickles 🥒
  • Coleslaw or lettuce 🥬
  • Spicy mayo (mayo + sriracha)
  • Oil for frying
  1. 1Marinate chicken thigh in buttermilk + hot sauce for at least 1 hr (overnight is perfect). 🍗
  2. 2Mix flour, cornstarch (50/50), paprika, garlic powder, cayenne, salt & pepper 🧂 in a bowl. This is your breading.
  3. 3Dredge marinated chicken in the flour mix. Press hard so coating sticks. Let rest 5 mins.
  4. 4Fry in oil at 350°F for 6-7 mins per side until dark golden brown and internal temp hits 165°F.
  5. 5Toast brioche bun 🍔. Spread spicy mayo. Add pickles 🥒, lettuce 🥬, and that crispy legend of a chicken piece. 🏆
💡 Daiiv's Tip: Cornstarch is what makes it extra crispy. Don't skip it. And don't crowd the oil — one piece at a time if needed.
